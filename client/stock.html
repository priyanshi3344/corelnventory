Operation Workspace

Each validation updates stock and writes to move history.

The flow below follows the document: create the document, fill product details, and validate the movement.

1. Receipts

Incoming

Use when items arrive from vendors. Validate to increase stock automatically.

  1. Create a receipt
  2. Add supplier and product
  3. Input received quantity
  4. Validate receipt

2. Delivery Orders

Outgoing

Pick and pack stock for shipment. Validation decreases stock from the selected source location.

  1. Pick items from stock
  2. Pack items for shipment
  3. Validate delivery order

3. Internal Transfers

Internal

Move stock between company locations. Each transfer is logged in the ledger.

  • Main Warehouse -> Production Floor
  • Rack A -> Rack B
  • Warehouse 1 -> Warehouse 2

4. Stock Adjustments

Audit

Fix mismatches between physical and recorded stock. Reason and operator stay on the audit trail.

  • Select product and location
  • Enter positive or negative adjustment quantity
  • Validate adjustment
