Operations

Receipts, Delivery Orders, Inventory Adjustment, and Internal Transfers.

Open Move History

1. Receipts (Incoming Goods)

Used when items arrive from vendors.

  1. Create receipt
  2. Add supplier and products
  3. Input received quantity
  4. Validate to increase stock
Example: Receive 50 units of Steel Rods -> stock +50.

2. Delivery Orders (Outgoing Goods)

Used when stock leaves warehouse for shipment.

  1. Pick items
  2. Pack items
  3. Validate to decrease stock
Example: Sales order 10 chairs -> stock -10.

3. Internal Transfers

Move stock between locations inside company.

  • Main Warehouse -> Production Floor
  • Rack A -> Rack B
  • Warehouse 1 -> Warehouse 2
Every movement is logged in move ledger.

4. Stock Adjustments

Fix mismatch between recorded and physical stock.

  • Select product/location
  • Enter counted quantity
  • System updates stock and logs adjustment
Includes reason and operator for audit trail.
