Simplified Example Flow

Receive -> Move -> Deliver -> Adjust

Step 1

Receive Goods from Vendor

Example: Receive 100 kg steel -> stock +100

Step 2

Move to Production Rack

Internal transfer Main Store -> Production Rack, total stock unchanged.

Step 3

Deliver Finished Goods

Example: Deliver 20 units -> stock -20 from dispatch location.

Step 4

Adjust Damaged Items

Example: 3 kg damaged -> stock -3, logged to stock ledger.

Additional Features

Low stock alerts, multi-warehouse support, SKU search, and smart filters.
