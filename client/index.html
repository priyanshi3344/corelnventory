Simplified Example Workflow

Receive → Move → Deliver → Adjust

Step 1

Receive Goods from Vendor

Example: Receive 500 kg steel coil from vendor — stock +500 kg, lot tracked.

Step 2

Move to Production Rack

Move steel from Main Store to Cutting Floor — location updated, total stock unchanged.

Step 3

Deliver Finished Goods

Example: Dispatch 20 aluminium sheets to client — stock -20, delivery order logged.

Step 4

Adjust Damaged Items

Example: 3 kg damaged during handling — stock -3, reason logged to ledger.
